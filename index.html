    How to Play

    OBJECTIVE

    PLAYERS MUST FIND AND SUBMIT A LIST OF 10 VALID WORDS THAT ACHIEVES THE HIGHEST CUMULATIVE SCORE OF THOSE 10 WORDS. WORDS ARE FORMED BY CHAINING 4+ TILES TOGETHER WITH EACH TILE CONNECTED TO AN ADJACENT NEIGHBOR TILE OF THE ONE THAT CAME BEFORE

    TILE VALUES

    EACH TILE STARTS WITH A STANDARD BASE POINT VALUE

    • 2 points: D, G
    • 3 points: B, C, M, P
    • 4 points: F, H, V, W, Y
    • 5 points: K

    THE PHRASE PANEL

    Tap the PHRASE tab on the right side of the board to open the Phrase Panel.

    In DAILY MODE, two secret anagram phrases are hidden across the letter grid. Both phrases share the exact same pool of letters — they are perfect anagrams of each other.

    • FIND A PHRASE: Swipe the letters of a phrase in sequence and press SUBMIT. You don't need to use the exact tiles the board placed — just spell the letters in the correct order.
    • AUTO-REVEAL: When you find a phrase, it lights up in the Phrase Panel and its hint button is disabled — you no longer need it.
    • PHRASE BONUS: Find BOTH phrases before submitting your word list to unlock the Phrase Bonus — a pool of up to 2000 bonus points added directly to your final score.

    THE HINTS

    Each daily board comes with 3 hints — but they are not created equal.

    PHRASE HINTS (×2)

    Each phrase has its own written clue — a composite metaphor where every element maps back to an individual word in the phrase. Your job is to deconstruct the metaphor: break apart the imagery, identify what each piece represents, and reconstruct the hidden words.

    Example: "A skin-suited briefcase carrying an alarm clock set for sunrise."
    → skin-suited briefcase = MAN    → alarm clock set for sunrise = ROOSTER
    → Phrase: MAN ROOSTER

    COMMUNAL LAYOUT HINT (×1)

    Reveals the word-count and letter-by-letter structure for BOTH phrases simultaneously, using dashes:

    ---- --- ------  &  -- ---- ------

    This tells you exactly how many words each phrase contains and how long each word is — but not the letters themselves. Because it unlocks structural information about both phrases at once, it carries the heaviest bonus deduction of all three hints.

    USE HINTS SPARINGLY — each one chips into your Phrase Bonus pool.

    THE PHRASE BONUS

    Find BOTH phrases to unlock a bonus pool of up to 2000 points added directly to your final score.

    The bonus starts at its maximum. Each hint you spend deducts from it:

    • PHRASE 1 HINT: −500 pts
    • PHRASE 2 HINT: −500 pts
    • LAYOUT HINT: −800 pts

    ORDER MATTERS — the earlier a hint is used, the larger the deduction (1st ×1.5, 2nd ×1.2, 3rd ×1.0).

    • No hints used → +2000 pts
    • Layout hint (1st): 2000 − (800×1.5) = +800 pts
    • Phrase 1 (1st): 2000 − (500×1.5) = +1250 pts
    • All 3 hints → bonus reduced to 0

    The live bonus preview is shown in the Phrase Panel at all times — check it before spending a hint.

    STRATEGY TIPS

    • PRIORITIZE LONG WORDS: THE LENGTH MULTIPLIER GIVES A HEFTY BOOST TO WORDS OF 5 LETTERS OR MORE
    • LOOK FOR REUSE OPPORTUNITIES: TAKE ADVANTAGE OF REUSING AND MAXING OUT TILES TO REAP THE FULL REWARDS
    • POOL OF POTENTIAL: YOUR WORD LIST PANEL COMES WITH AN IN GAME WORD SCORING CALCULATOR THAT WILL HELP YOU DETERMINE WHICH WORDS TO KEEP/REMOVE

    How to Play — Hexacore

    OBJECTIVE

    HEXACORE IS AN ENDLESS SURVIVAL MODE. CHAIN LETTER TILES TO SPELL VALID WORDS AND EARN POINTS. WORDS ARE AUTO-SUBMITTED WHEN YOU RELEASE YOUR DRAG. THE GAME ENDS WHEN AN EMBER TILE REACHES THE BOTTOM OF THE GRID.

    HOW TO SPELL A WORD

    DRAG across adjacent hex tiles to chain letters into a word. You must chain at least 4 letters. Release to auto-submit. You can backtrack by dragging back to the previous tile to deselect it.

    Digraph tiles (two-letter tiles like HE, EN, TH) count as 2 letters each toward your word length.

    SCORING

    Each tile has a base point value:

    • 1 pt: A, E, I, O, U, L, N, R, S, T
    • 2 pts: D, G
    • 3 pts: B, C, M, P
    • 4 pts: F, H, V, W, Y
    • 5 pts: K
    • 8 pts: J, X
    • 10 pts: Q, Z

    Plus word length multipliers for 5+ letters:

    • 5 letters: ×3
    • 6 letters: ×4
    • 7 letters: ×5
    • 8 letters: ×6
    • 9 letters: ×7
    • 10+ letters: ×10

    SPECIAL TILES

    EMBER
    PRISM
    RUNE
    DIGRAPH
    • 🔥 EMBER — Spawns every 3 words in the top row. Advances downward after each word. If it reaches the bottom — game over. Consume it in a word to destroy it. Using an Ember also rewards bonus gems equal to the word length bonus.
    • ✦ PRISM — Doubles the word score (×2). Spawns every 5 words.
    • ✦ RUNE — A wildcard. Tap it first to choose any letter, then chain it in your word. Spawns every 7 words.
    • ❋ DIGRAPH — A double-letter tile. Both letters contribute their point values. Counts as 2 letters toward word length.

    GEM TILES

    Longer words reward rarer gems that multiply your score. Gems stack multiplicatively.

    ×2 EMERALD
    ×3 GOLD
    ×4 SAPPHIRE
    ×5 PEARL
    ×6 TANZANITE
    ×7 RUBY
    ×LEN DIAMOND
    • ◆ EMERALD (×2) — 4-letter words
    • ◆ GOLD (×3) — 6-letter words
    • ◆ SAPPHIRE (×4) — 7-letter words
    • ◆ PEARL (×5) — 8-letter words
    • ◆ TANZANITE (×6) — 9-letter words
    • ◆ RUBY (×7) — 10+ letter words
    • ◆ DIAMOND (×word length) — rare, extremely powerful

    Using multiple gems of the same type gives a count bonus (e.g. 3 Emeralds → ×6 instead of ×2).

    PORTALS

    ENTRY ◈
    EXIT ◉

    After every 10 words submitted, two corner tiles transform into a portal pair — one entry (◈) and one exit (◉). Chain through both in the same word to link non-adjacent tiles. The portal closes after 3 words or when a portal tile is consumed.

    STRATEGY TIPS

    • 🔥 DESTROY EMBERS EARLY — Consume ember tiles in words before they reach the bottom.
